About Us

welcome to Raj Pharmacy & Health Store

Situated in Zuzuvadi, Hosur, Tamil Nadu, Raj Pharmacy & Health Store has been a steadfast provider of premium healthcare solutions since our inception in 2021.

Founder Profile

Mr. Survindraj, the visionary behind our establishment, is a distinguished professional with a Bachelor's degree in Pharmacy and an MBA in Hospital Management. His commitment to excellence and passion for healthcare drives our commitment to enhancing the well-being of our community.

At Raj Pharmacy & Health Store, we transcend the conventional role of a pharmacy. Our mission is to deliver not just medications but a comprehensive healthcare experience. We understand the significance of your health and are dedicated to offering a range of pharmaceuticals, wellness products, and expert guidance.

Your health is our priority, and we express our gratitude for entrusting Raj Pharmacy & Health Store with your healthcare needs. Experience the pinnacle of professional healthcare services with us.

Survindraj

Owner of Raj Pharmacy And Health Store
